We are experiencing what Gallup call's 'an emplyee engagment crisis'. Just one.third of people at work are engaged.

To improve performance and engagment companies rely on the same principals as industrial-age factories. But all those annual performance reviews and hundred-questions surveys don't work anymore. People want regular feedback. Leaders who don't embrace this change end up with misaligned, disengaged and dwindling team.

We designed Teambit to change that. It empowers teams to make feedback a daily habit. Everybody knows what they are doing well and what they need to improve, leading to better performing, engaged and happier teams.
